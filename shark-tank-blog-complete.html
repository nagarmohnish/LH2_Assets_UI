Shark Tank Episodes

Welcome to The Shark Tank Blog's Shark Tank episodes page. All Shark Tank episodes are listed by season, episode number and original broadcast date. Click each season for a complete list of Shark Tank episodes.

Shark Tank first premiered in 2009 and has since become one of the most popular reality TV shows in the United States. The show features aspiring entrepreneurs pitching their business ideas to a panel of investors known as "Sharks."

Season 1 had 14 episodes, Season 2 had 9, Season 3 had 15, Season 4 had 26, and Seasons 5, 6, and 7 each had 28. During Season 6, the show aired its 100th episode.

The success of Shark Tank led to Beyond the Tank, a spinoff that followed up on entrepreneurs who had previously appeared on the show.

Shark Tank Episodes on CNBC

Since January 2014, CNBC has aired syndicated reruns of Shark Tank. With Season 17 on air, there are hundreds of episodes available for reruns. The show grew from a niche audience into a Friday night ratings powerhouse by Season 3.

Shark Tank Season 17

Season 17 premiered on September 24, 2025, continuing as one of the most successful business shows on television. More than 1,000 businesses have appeared on the show over 16+ seasons. Over 40,000 businesses apply every year, proving that the momentum is getting stronger with each passing season.
